Top 5 AI Engineering Tools for Audio Producers

January 26, 2026

As a self-taught engineer, I'm always looking for ways to streamline my workflow in Reason and Mixcraft. These five AI-powered tools are changing the game for home studios.

  • Lalal.ai: Exceptional for stem separation. It allows you to pull clean vocals out of a mixed track so you can re-engineer the "vocal pocket."
  • RipX Daw: An AI-first DAW that treats audio like MIDI, allowing for surgical-level editing of individual notes within a flattened audio file.
  • Landr: An AI mastering suite that provides a solid foundation for your final levels without needing a $10,000 outboard gear setup.
  • iZotope Ozone: Their "Master Assistant" uses machine learning to suggest EQ and compression settings based on your reference track.
  • Sonible smart:EQ: An equalizer that "learns" your audio and automatically carves out space to prevent frequency masking.
