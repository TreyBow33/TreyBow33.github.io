Technical Analysis

Using Mixcraft to analyze these tracks, we utilized fade-ins and fade-outs to isolate specific vocal transients. Our observation found that the AI version lacks the micro-imperfections in timing—known as rubato—that characterize human performances. While the AI successfully replicates the vocal timbre and formant structure, the "soul" of the track is found in the natural, unquantized rhythms of the human-produced version.
